Our Services

Property Sale/Purchase

With years of experience in identifying properties with strong growth potential, we specialise in acquiring real estate at the right price — before value surges. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, or evaluate a property, our team provides expert guidance, market insights, and end-to-end support to ensure every transaction is a smart one.

Real estate consultancy

Our consultancy service is designed to provide you with expert guidance at every stage of your real estate journey. Whether you're buying, selling, investing, or developing property, we offer tailored advice based on in-depth market knowledge and years of experience. We help you make informed decisions, avoid costly mistakes, and maximise the value of your investment.

Property Development

We understand how challenging it can be to determine the right type of development for a particular property. With our extensive experience, we offer tailored advice specific to each location and work alongside you to ensure the development aligns with your vision and goals. Whether it's planning, layout, property development, or renovation, we're here to guide and support you every step of the way.

Property Management

Our Property Management service takes the stress out of owning property. Whether you're away or simply prefer not to deal with the day-to-day responsibilities, we handle everything — from tenant communication and rent collection to maintenance and inspections — so you can enjoy the benefits of ownership without the hassle

Land Survey

Our surveying service at J.ola Real Estate provides accurate, legally recognized measurements essential for property acquisition, development, and dispute resolution. Our licensed surveyors utilize modern equipment to conduct boundary surveys, topographic mapping, construction layout, subdivision planning, title verification, and cadastral surveys. These services help clients clearly define property lines, comply with zoning regulations, prepare sites for construction, and secure vital legal documentation such as Certificates of Occupancy (C of O)

Lease Agreement & Documentation

Navigating lease agreements can be complex and time-consuming. At J.ola Real Estate, we provide expert lease negotiation and documentation services to protect your interests. Our experienced team carefully reviews, drafts, and negotiates lease terms to ensure clarity, fairness, and compliance with local laws. We handle everything from rent terms and maintenance responsibilities to renewal options and dispute resolution clauses, helping you avoid costly misunderstandings and legal issues.

About us

Hon. Jayeola Toriola

J.ola Toriola Real Estate Agency is a registered property firm in Nigeria (CRBN 332307), registerd in 2014. With over 5,000 satisfied clients and more than 7,000 completed transactions, we have earned a solid reputation for delivering trusted, transparent, and client-focused real estate services across the country — with a primary focus in Lagos State and the capacity to serve clients nationwide.

Led by Hon. Jayeola Toriola, a two-time Chairman of Estate Rent And Commision Agents Association of Nigeria (ERCAAN) Mainland Zone, Lagos .Our firm is built on a foundation of professionalism, industry leadership, and a commitment to ethical real estate practice in Nigeria.

Our team offers comprehensive expertise across all property types — including residential, commercial, industrial, and mixed-use developments. From land acquisition and property development to investment advisory and property management, we serve private individuals, investors, developers, and institutions with tailored real estate solutions that meet both immediate needs and long-term goals.

While we do not provide financing, we offer dedicated legal advisory services to support safe, compliant, and well-documented real estate transactions — helping clients navigate land titles, ownership, zoning regulations, and contract agreements with confidence.

At J.ola Real Estate, we are driven by core values of integrity, innovation, and excellence. Our mission is to make real estate accessible, secure, and rewarding for every client we serve. Whether you're buying your first plot, expanding your investment portfolio, or developing large-scale projects, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
